FH6 Random Car — Control Panel

Set which cars are eligible below, then click Save filters. Any Channel Points redemption of your configured reward (or the manual Spin button here) will pick randomly from cars matching these filters. Leave a group empty to allow every option in that group.

Leave every group empty and hit Save to reset to "pick randomly, but manufacturer-first" — a random manufacturer is chosen, then a random car from that manufacturer, rather than any of 600+ cars uniformly.

Chat can also trigger spins directly, independent of the filters above: !changecar (manufacturer-first random), or !changecar-<word> where <word> is a manufacturer (!changecar-honda), country (!changecar-japan), class (!changecar-s1), drivetrain (!changecar-rwd), or decade (!changecar-90s).
