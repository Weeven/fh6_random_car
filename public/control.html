FH6 Random Car — Control Panel

Set which cars are eligible below, then click Save filters. Every spin picks a random manufacturer — not a specific car model — from whatever matches these filters, so you pick which car of that manufacturer to drive in-game. Leave a group empty to allow every option in that group.

Leave every group empty and hit Save to reset to "any manufacturer" — every eligible manufacturer gets equal odds, regardless of how many cars they have in the game (so a 1-car manufacturer isn't drowned out by one with 30, e.g. Ford).

Chat can also trigger spins directly, independent of the filters above: !changecar (any manufacturer), or !changecar-<word> where <word> is a region (!changecar-asia), country (!changecar-japan), manufacturer (!changecar-honda), class (!changecar-s1), drivetrain (!changecar-rwd), or decade (!changecar-90s). Every variant reveals a manufacturer only, same as the Spin button.
