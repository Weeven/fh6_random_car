FH6 Random Car — Control Panel

Pick filters below, then click Save filters. A spin reveals exactly as much as you've filtered down to — never a specific car model. Pick just Class C and it shows "Class C". Pick Nissan + Class A and it shows "Nissan · Class A". You then pick whichever matching car you want to drive in-game. If a combo you've picked matches too few cars, the reveal automatically backs off to a slightly broader combo instead.

Leave every group empty and hit Save to reset to "any manufacturer" — a random manufacturer is revealed, with every manufacturer getting equal odds regardless of how many cars they have in the game (so a 1-car manufacturer isn't drowned out by one with 30, e.g. Ford).

Chat can also trigger spins directly, independent of the filters above: !changecar (any manufacturer), or !changecar-<word> where <word> is a country (!changecar-japan), manufacturer (!changecar-honda), class (!changecar-s1), drivetrain (!changecar-rwd), decade (!changecar-90s), or division (!changecar-trackToys, !changecar-rallymonsters). Each reveals exactly that one thing, same rules as above.
